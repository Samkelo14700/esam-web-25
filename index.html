Our Vision

We envision a vibrant, connected community of Eswatini students thriving academically and personally in Morocco while keeping strong tiers to our homeland and culture.

Our Mission

The Eswatini Student Association of Morocco (ESAM) aims to provide a supportive community for students from Eswatini (formerly Swaziland) studying in Morocco. We work to maintain cultural connections, facilitate academic success, and create networking opportunities.
